# Spells n'' Gods – Foundational Pantheon

This document defines the **finalized Spells n'' Gods pantheon** for the Spells n'' Gods mod.  
It includes domains, gender balance, rivalries, worship mechanics, and offering doctrine.

---

## Divine Binding Rules

- Each player may **choose only one god**.
- Divine selection occurs **once**, via binding.
- Binding is performed by right-clicking an armor stand with a **Rune of [God]**.
- The armor stand is consumed and replaced with a **Monument of [God]**:
  - Uses player skin (configurable fallback)
  - Acts as the sole interaction point for prayer and offerings
- Changing gods (apostasy) is possible but **extremely punishing**, involving:
  - Monument desecration
  - Permanent penalties
  - Extended cooldowns
  - New god begins at negative favor

---

## Worship Loop

### Prayer
- Prevents favor decay
- Scales with time, not spam
- Can be interrupted or weakened by rival influence

### Offerings
- Required to maintain blessings
- **72 real-world hours** without a valid offering halts blessings
- Rival god effects and curses persist regardless
- Restoring offerings resumes blessings immediately

## The Pantheon (4 Gods, 4 Goddesses)

---

## AUREX – God of Creation & Form (Male)
**Domain:** Crafting, structure, permanence, matter

Aurex governs:
- Smithing, enchanting, construction
- Item durability, refinement, quality
- Structures, machines, artifacts

Favor Rewards:
- Flawless crafting chances
- Reduced material waste
- Increased durability

Wrath:
- Item defects
- Accelerated degradation
- Structural failures

**Offering Doctrine:**  
Master-crafted, unused items. No mass production.

**Rivals:**  
Primary: Umbriel  
Secondary: Chronyx

**Philosophy:** *What is made must endure.*

---

## NYXARA – Goddess of Magic & Arcana (Female)
**Domain:** Spells, mana, knowledge, the unseen

Nyxara governs:
- Spellcasting and rituals
- Mana flow and mutation
- Magical research

Favor Rewards:
- Reduced mana costs
- Enhanced spell behavior
- Forbidden glyph access

Wrath:
- Spell backlash
- Mana instability
- Arcane corruption

**Offering Doctrine:**  
Mana expenditure, scrolls, drained crystals, risky rituals.

**Rivals:**  
Primary: Meridian  
Secondary: Mortyss

**Philosophy:** *Power is curiosity made dangerous.*

---

## KHELR – God of War & Strife (Male)
**Domain:** Combat, dominance, honor, conflict

Khelr governs:
- PvE and PvP combat
- Tactics, morale, leadership
- Weapon mastery

Favor Rewards:
- Scaling combat buffs
- Rally effects
- Enhanced drops

Wrath:
- Cowardice debuffs
- Weapon failure
- Combat penalties

**Offering Doctrine:**  
Trophies from honorable, direct combat only.

**Rivals:**  
Primary: Viren  
Secondary: Meridian

**Philosophy:** *Peace is earned only through strength.*
